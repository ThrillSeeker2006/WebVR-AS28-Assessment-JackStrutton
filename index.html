General Information:

Parkour is an athletic training discipline founded in the late 1990s which blew up on social media in recent years. The goal of Parkour is to get from point A to point B in the fastest and most efficient way possible, without equipment assisting the traceur. Tracuers also perform flips to make an effort to get extra brownie points in competitions, for fun, or to show off to friends or other fellow traceurs whilst training. Parkour has spawned new sub-sports known as tricking and freerunning. Parkour and freerunning's names can be used interchangeably as they are fundimentally with very little differences, though Parkour is the predominant sport.
